Below is the complete overview of weeks, topics, objectives, and assessments.

Week Chapter Topic Course Objectives SLOs Activities Assessments
Week 1 Course Syllabus Introduction, syllabus review Students can select interesting biological topics. Introduction, syllabus review
Week 2 The Scientific Method Basics of the scientific method and experimental design Teach principles of the scientific method and hypothesis formation Formulate hypotheses; identify variables. Discussion on students' selected topics, article selection and database use for references The students practice searching articles related to the topic of interest.
Week 3 Finding Information About Topics in Science How to research and evaluate scientific sources Develop research skills to find and assess credible sources Locate and assess reliable scientific sources Scavenger hunts for articles; compare sources Quiz; submit experiment designs, evaluate sources, and submit bibliography.
Week 4 Reading and Writing About Statistical Analyses Understanding and writing about statistical data Train students to interpret and write about statistics Analyze and interpret statistical results Analyze datasets and improve graph design Write data analysis reports and graphs
Week 5 Documenting Sources Proper citation and avoiding plagiarism Teach proper citation practices and plagiarism prevention Cite sources correctly; avoid plagiarism Practice formatting citations; discuss plagiarism Submit citation exercises; plagiarism response.
Students should annotate at least 5 articles, of which at least 2 articles will be used for presentation.

Week 7 Data Analysis Using Statistics Statistical data analysis and presentation Enable accurate analysis and presentation of statistical data Perform and present statistical analyses Select statistical tests and graph analysis Submit statistical analyses and graphs
Week 8 and 9 Editing and improving scientific documents Enhance revision skills for scientific writing Revise drafts for clarity and accuracy Edit texts; provide peer feedback Submit revised drafts; use feedback checklists

Week 1 Details
Topic: Course Syllabus
Course Objectives: Introduction, syllabus review
SLOs: Students can select interesting biological topics.
Activities: Introduction, syllabus review
Assessments: (none listed)
Week 2 Details
Chapter: The Scientific Method
Topic: Basics of the scientific method and experimental design.
Course Objectives: Teach principles of the scientific method and hypothesis formation.
SLOs: Formulate hypotheses; identify variables.
Activities: Discussion on students' selected topics, with the instructor demonstrating article selection and database use for references.
Assessments: The students practice searching articles related to the topic of interest.
Week 3 Details
Chapter: Finding Information About Topics in Science
Topic: How to research and evaluate scientific sources.
Course Objectives: Develop research skills to find and assess credible sources.
SLOs: Locate and assess reliable scientific sources.
Activities: Scavenger hunts for articles; compare sources.
Assessments: Quiz; submit experiment designs, evaluate sources, and submit bibliography.
Week 4 Details
Chapter: Reading and Writing About Statistical Analyses
Topic: Understanding and writing about statistical data.
Course Objectives: Train students to interpret and write about statistics.
SLOs: Analyze and interpret statistical results.
Activities: Analyze datasets and improve graph design.
Assessments: Write data analysis reports and graphs.
Week 5 Details
Chapter: Documenting Sources
Topic: Proper citation and avoiding plagiarism.
Course Objectives: Teach proper citation practices and plagiarism prevention.
SLOs: Cite sources correctly; avoid plagiarism.
Activities: Practice formatting citations; discuss plagiarism.
Assessments: Submit citation exercises; plagiarism response.
Students should annotate at least 5 articles, of which at least 2 articles will be focused on for presentation.
Week 6 Details
Chapter: Preparing a Scientific Paper
Topic: Step-by-step guidance for scientific writing.
Course Objectives: Guide students to write structured scientific papers.
SLOs: Write clear, organized scientific papers.
Activities: Create outlines and peer review sections.
Assessments: Draft paper; Quiz on paper structure.
Week 7 Details
Chapter: Data Analysis Using Statistics
Topic: Statistical data analysis and presentation.
Course Objectives: Enable accurate analysis and presentation of statistical data.
SLOs: Perform and present statistical analyses.
Activities: Select statistical tests and graph analysis.
Assessments: Submit statistical analyses and graphs.
Week 8 Details
Topic: Editing and improving scientific documents.
Course Objectives: Enhance revision skills for scientific writing.
SLOs: Revise drafts for clarity and accuracy.
Activities: Edit texts; provide peer feedback.
Assessments: Submit revised drafts; use feedback checklists.
